What is Backtesting?
Backtesting lets you test a trading strategy on past data before risking real money. Pick a stock, choose a strategy, set your time period and starting capital — and see exactly how much you would have made (or lost). All results use real NSE historical prices via yfinance.
① Pick a Stock
Type any NSE symbol or company name
② Choose a Strategy
🚀 Momentum
RSI mean reversion: buy when deeply oversold (RSI < 33), sell when overbought (RSI > 65).
🤖 AI Score
Multi-factor score: RSI + MACD + price momentum + trend. Buy ≥ 62, sell ≤ 40.
📈 SMA Crossover
Buy when 20-day SMA crosses 50-day SMA. Simple but effective short-term swing strategy.
🏦 Buy & Hold
Buy once and hold forever. Buffett's favourite. Baseline to beat with any other strategy.

What do these numbers mean?
CAGR — Compounded Annual Growth Rate. How much your money grows each year on average.
Sharpe Ratio — Return per unit of risk. Above 1 = good, above 2 = excellent.
Max Drawdown — Worst drop from peak to trough. How much you could have lost at the worst time.
Win Rate — % of trades that made money. Even 50% can be profitable with the right strategy.
